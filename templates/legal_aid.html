National Legal Services Authority (NALSA)

Under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987, NALSA provides FREE legal services to marginalized and economically weak citizens. If your income is below ₹1,00,000 per year, you are entitled to a free lawyer, free legal advice, and Lok Adalat services.
